B's Spicery

A functioning back end for a dummy online store

A fully functioning backend CRUD application for a dummy online store. The database includes fake users, products, reviews and recipes. Endpoints allow creation of users, products, recipes and reviews, listing all products, recipes and even adding to and removing from a customer basket.

This project was born from a discussion with my partner about the potential to open a spice and recipe kit store locally as there are currently no suppliers in our area. With an aim to make pan Asian cooking less intimidating and more accessible at home. A functioning front end is in the works for when this server is complete.
