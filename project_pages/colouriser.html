Colouriser

An app designed for testing the quality of paper samples

I custom built this single page application with just vanilla JavaScript, html and css. Initially designed for use with tablets.

The brief was to create an application that could be used on a tablet with an accompanying microscope. The user would place paper samples over the coloured box and view the sample through a microscope. By using different colours and the variable percentages, the user can inspect the sample for defects at the research and development phase of creating various types of paper.
